ABOUT US

Team Inferno DTU is a technical team which was founded in September 2013. In 2017, the team reinvented itself, redirecting its boundless creativity, technical expertise and experience gained from previous successful ventures, into building rovers for exploring the terrain of Mars, and participating in various rover challenges.

Mars has piqued the interest of mankind ever since the launch of Apollo 11. While manned missions to Mars are yet to be realised, manmade rovers are already traversing the surface of Mars. Planetary exploration and unmanned rovers are fields with virtually limitless scope for expansion and Team Inferno hopes to push the boundaries of these fields. The path to progress is forged by the pursuit of the unknown. With this principle in mind, Team Inferno forayed into the field of rovers and robotics, swiftly establishing its mark. While building a rover that could withstand the harsh and unruly climate of Mars seemed like a daunting task at first, Team Inferno was more than up to it.

Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch of engineering and science that includes mechanical engineering, electronics engineering, information engineering, computer science, and others. The enormously talented students of Team Inferno DTU came together from various disciplines to work in the field of robotics and build the first Martian Rover prototype of Delhi Technological University.

The mechanical team had to implement advance manufacturing techniques to ensure accuracy and durability. The electrical team was entrusted with linking all the sub systems present on the rover, while at the same time ensuring high accuracy and low latency. The software team worked on the autonomy of the rover, implementing a myriad of technologies and software in the rover, for autonomy, obstacle detection and avoidance.
